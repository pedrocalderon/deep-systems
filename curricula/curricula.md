# Curricula

This section of the project is used to layout the work to be done.

Conceptually any new tech subject I would like to study deeply would be added as
subfolder here, and it should be broken down further in multiple study blocks,
creating clear goals and highlighting progress.

I'm experimenting with this format, since studying alone has always been hard
for me, since I tend to shift interests and thus loose focus.

The study plan and the exercises I do are expected to leave here, while the
study notes will leave in a flat structure in the root directory for easy of
consumption in the future.
